A small web-app that went viral

March 2024 •

30,000+ student visits. 15,000+ marksheets generated. All within 2 days of launch. Numbers that left me speechless, but they're not the real story here. The real story is about dreams, sticky notes, and the power of visualization.

I'm, a Chartered Accountant who finds joy in coding. Two years ago, I wrote "95 in costing" on a simple desk note—a quiet promise to myself. While I didn't hit that exact number, I ended up scoring higher than the All India Rank 1 of my attempt. Sometimes, life surprises you in the best ways possible.

Over the years, I've watched my juniors embrace this same practice. One stuck her target marks on the fridge; another made his goal his phone's lockscreen. Today, they both wear the CA badge with pride. It got me thinking: small dreams don't create CAs—bold ones do.

That's why I built this web app (caresults.in) —a simple tool to help CA students visualize their success. It's free, will always be. No ads, no sign-ups, no complications. Just a clean space where you can craft your future marksheet and hold your ambitions in your hands. Because sometimes, seeing is believing.

Having walked the CA path, I know intimately about the late nights, the self-doubt, and the weight of these exams. If this small tool helps lift even a fraction of that burden—if it helps one student see their potential more clearly—then every minute spent building it was worth it.

To every CA student reading this: You're built for more than just passing; you're built for excellence. Make your dreams tangible, one mark at a time. And remember, somewhere out there, a fellow CA is rooting for your success.
